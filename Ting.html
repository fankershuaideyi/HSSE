Tinghua university

Tsinghua University (Tsinghua for short), located in Haidian District, Beijing, is a national key university directly under the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China. "Tsinghua University is one of the top universities in China. The "Strong Foundation Plan", "111 Plan", the "Nine Schools Alliance (C9)" [89], the "Pine Alliance", the "China Association of University Presidents", the "Asian University Consortium", the "Pacific Rim University Consortium", the "China-Russia Comprehensive University Consortium" [70], the "Tsinghua-MIT Low Carbon University Consortium", and the "China University of Science and Technology". It is a member of the Cambridge-MIT Low Carbon University Consortium, a base for training high-level talents and scientific and technological research in China, and is known as the "cradle of red engineers".
